The Heritage

Our Legacy

Welcome to Reha’s, where a dream has blossomed into reality. From a young age, Reha was captivated by the art of beauty, seeing it as a form of engineering. Growing up amidst the breathtaking landscapes of Srinagar, she absorbed the natural beauty around her, which became an integral part of her being.

Reha's dedication to her skincare routine was meticulous, always ensuring she looked her best. This inner passion evolved into a full-fledged career in the beauty industry, making her an unstoppable force today.
